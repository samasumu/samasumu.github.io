Plants

Fittonia

Fittonia is a beautiful plant that is relatively easy to maintain. The plant will literally collapse if it is dried out. If this does happen, be sure to thoroughly water and it will recover quickly. Fittonia dislike strong, direct sunlight and should be kept moist.

Hosta

Hosta is traditionally grown outside but can make wonderful houseplants if you're up for the challenge. This plant grows best in the shade, needs lots of water, and has a dormant period in the winter.

Moses in the Craddle

Moses in the Craddle is known for its stunning colors and makes for a beautiful house plant year-round. This plant needs light year-round and will bloom any time of year. It can still be in lower light but may lose color.
